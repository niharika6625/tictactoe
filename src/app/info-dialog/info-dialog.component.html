Game Information & Features

Rules:

  • Game always starts with player X.
  • Players take turns to place X or O on the grid.
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is declared a draw.

Features included in the assignment:

  • A sound plays when a player places X or O
  • Winner sound and visual highlight for winning tiles.
  • The game keeps track of wins for both players and draws.
  • Restart game and clear score features.
