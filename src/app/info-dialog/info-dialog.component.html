Game Information

Rules:

  • Players take turns to place X or O on the grid.
  • First to align three marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
  • If all tiles are filled and there's no winner, the game is a draw.

Features:

  • Sound effects for each player's move.
  • Winner sound and visual highlight for winning tiles.
  • Score tracking and result history (X wins, O wins, Draws).
  • Reset game and clear history features.
